About us

Not so long ago in order to get taxi, people had to stood in long que, then caching taxi evolved into standing at street and rising hand with thumb up. Nowadays you can still catch taxi with use of your thumb, by touching yout smartphone screen. Just run our app and check why is it worth to use it.

The main aim of our app is to allow everyone to catch taxi, wherever and whenever he or she needs it. In order to make it possible we have created one of the biggest cab fleets in country. Our taxis are present in each bigger city, with thousands of professional drivers.

When you need transporation services, you just turn on our app, click button and soon one of our drivers start heading your way. You don't even need to know where you are. Our app allows you to stay in direct contact with driver from the moment you have pushed button on screen till cab arrive your location.

Each ride is registerd in your account, so you don't have to worry about honesty of the bill and our employees are always ready to help in checking the way and calculation.
